About Us

We are Relax Volleyball Team , a group of mainly former volleyball players, lovers, and families who are passionate about the sport and building strong relationships through it. Our team was established in 2010 with the aim of promoting volleyball and providing a platform for people to come together and enjoy the sport in a fun and relaxed environment.

Our team is open to players of all levels, from beginners to experienced players. We have regular training sessions and also participate in local volleyball tournaments and events. Our team is not just about playing volleyball, but also about building lasting friendships and creating a supportive community to the society and country in As a whole .
